# Canvas

An infinite whiteboard for spatial planning and freeform notes inside Frontier.

Canvas gives you an endless, zoomable space to think on. Drop down sticky notes anywhere, sketch out colored areas to group them, and arrange everything by hand the way you'd lay cards out on a wall — for planning, brainstorming, mapping ideas, or just keeping loose notes somewhere you can see them all at once. Pan and zoom across the whole space, use the minimap to find your way around, and keep as many separate boards as you like. Everything you place stays exactly where you left it, ready when you come back.

## Features

- An infinite, zoomable, pannable canvas you open as its own tab
- Draggable sticky notes in a range of colors, with editable labels
- Colored areas (banner + notes) to group and organize what's on the board
- A minimap for getting around large boards at a glance
- As many named boards as you want, listed in the sidebar
- Your layout is saved automatically — positions, colors, and where you were looking

## Install

Install Canvas from the **Extensions → Marketplace** tab in Frontier: find Canvas, click Install, and the Canvas tab and sidebar appear (Frontier verifies the download before installing). No configuration needed.
